# Testing with AI
Using AI tools, test generation, copilot, agentic testing

## Trainee
- Understands what AI assistants and LLMs are, what prompts are, and why outputs are non-deterministic.
- Can use approved AI tools to ask testing questions and explain basic code or testing concepts.
- Understands basic risks: hallucinations, confidentiality, and the need to verify outputs.

## Junior
- Uses AI to draft simple test cases, checklists, test data, scripts, bug reports, and test documentation.
- Uses AI to analyze software requirements, user data, and scenarios.
- Uses AI to understand unfamiliar code, logs, or business domain concepts.
- Validates AI-generated artifacts before use and follows organizational policy for safe data handling.

## Middle
- Independently applies structured prompting techniques such as context provisioning, examples, decomposition, and response constraints.
- Uses AI in recurring QE workflows: scenario generation, data preparation, automation support, test maintenance, flaky test triage, and log/root-cause analysis.
- Evaluates AI tools for accuracy, privacy, cost, and practical fit for the team.
- Uses AI safely within approved workflows and can explain where human review is still required.

## Senior
- Designs an AI-augmented testing approach for a product or team.
- Customizes assistants, knowledge bases, or bounded agents for project context.
- Integrates AI-enabled checks into the delivery workflow where appropriate, such as triage, maintenance, failure analysis, or prioritization.
- Defines team guardrails for safe usage and mentors others.
- Measures impact using concrete outcomes such as cycle-time reduction, flake reduction, maintenance effort, or defect detection improvement.

## Expert
- Defines organization-wide strategy and standards for AI use in Quality Engineering.
- Selects, builds, or scales enterprise AI capabilities for QE.
- Establishes governance, security, measurement, and adoption patterns across teams.
- Drives innovation in agentic quality workflows with clear human oversight and risk controls.
